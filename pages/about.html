About us

Welcome to Vermon store. We pride ourselves on our commitment to producing the best donuts possible, using high-quality ingredients, an extraordinary attention to detail, and all with a passion that shines through to everything we make. We wanted to use the highest possible quality ingredients from top to bottom, from glaze to dough. And we didn't want just any old donuts - we wanted flavors that would cause excitement, get people talking, and push the boundaries of what's expected. We craft our donuts fresh by hand, every single day. We're constantly experimenting and looking for that next big flavor. We love what we do and we want you to love our donuts as much as we love making them for you.
